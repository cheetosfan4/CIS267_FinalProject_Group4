# CIS267_FinalProject_Group4
## GUIDE
### GOAL
Progress through the three dungeons by defeating enemies, collecting items, and beating the bosses


### CONTROLS
Use WASD or the arrow keys to move

Press F to use your dagger

Press Q to open chests

Press T to pause the game

Press R to open your inventory

Press E to equip items in your inventory, or use them in the overworld

Cheats: Press numbers 1-3 to travel between the 3 levels, and press H to fully heal yourself.


### ITEMS
Hammer: Bounces the player forward over pits and damages enemies

Cannon: Shoots a large cannon ball that breaks cracked blocks and damages enemies

Artifact Trifecta: Creates a large blast that breaks tough blocks, damages enemies, and can break shields. Obtained after collecting all three artifacts

Apple: Heals 2 HP when consumed

Star: Makes the player shift colors
